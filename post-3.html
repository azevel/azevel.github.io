How Did Russian GDP Grow in 2025?

March 30, 2026

I looked at the 1% GDP growth figure for Russia in 2025. Since mid 22, Rosstat have limited or classified certain economic datasets to prevent "sanctions-related vulnerabilities.". As a result a detailed breakdown of what makes up the GDP is harder to verify. However, I found the Industrial Production Index which is measuring the output of every factory, mine, and power plant and can say a fair bit.

Total manufacturing for the full year 2025 grew by 3.6%, and manufacturing output jumped by 7.8% compared to the previous December. The primary growth is in: Other Transport Equipment (+33.1%): (READ: Aircraft, tanks, and drones), Finished Metal Products (+15.9%): (READ: Artillery shells and armor plating) and Computers, Electronics, and Optics (+11.7%): (READ: Guidance systems and semiconductors).

The most telling data point is the Finished Metal Products category in December 2025: it saw a massive surge of 156.8% compared to just one month prior (November). While these three sectors skyrocketed, the rest of the industrial base struggled. Mining and coal to water supply and civilian manufacturing all declined. the government is the primary buyer, printing rubles to fund equipment like tanks, drones, and navigation systems that are immediately sent to the front and destroyed. Adds GDP but subtracts national wealth.
